FEMA Map Modernization Program

FluidClarity performed Flood Insurance Studies (FISs) at avarious locations in FEMA Region V. FluidClarity updated existing flood insrance rate maps (FIRMs) and developed new FIRMs for critical watersheds in the region. The scope of work involves topographic data collection, hydrologic studies, geographic information system (GIS)-driven watershed analysis, preparation of FIRMs, and community outreach in support of the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP).
