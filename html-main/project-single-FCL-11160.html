North Avenue Area Sewer Separation Evaluation

The Village of River Forest retained FluidClarity to hydraulically evaluate the North Avenue combined sewer system and to investigate potential improvement alternatives to improve the drainage and reduce flooding in the North Avenue sewer service area of the village. More than 400 properties are impacted by the deficiencies in the drainage system.

FluidClarity prepared an existing conditions hydraulic model, which included data gathered from the Village, the Village of Elmwood, and Galewood, and the MWRDGC. A SWMM model was compiled to evaluate the existing hydraulic properties of the system. Three proposed alternatives were presented and modeled. The constructability of the alternatives was evaluated, along with permitting requirements. A report was delivered to the Village which summarized the findings.
