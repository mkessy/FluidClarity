Bull Creek Watershed Flood Insurance Study

FluidClarity performed a detailed study to update the existing effective FIS for the 11-square-mile Bull Creek Watershed in Lake County, Illinois. The scope of work involved topographic data collection, hydrologic and hydraulic studies using HECRAS and HEC-HMS, GIS-driven watershed analysis, preparation of floodplain and floodway maps, preparation of digital FIRMS, and community outreach.

FluidClarity developed HECRAS and HEC-HMS models for the Bull Creek watershed as part of the flood insurance study. Tasks included calibration validation and simulation of design storms.
