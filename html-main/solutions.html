Our Services

FluidClarity's goal is to help our clients develop innovative and environmentally sound solutions. Our technical consulting services help our clients to articulate and bridge business and community problems, leading to greater organizational effectiveness and improved bottom-line results.

Civil & Transportation Engineering

Projects include roadways, storm and sanitary sewer improvements, flood storage reservoirs, pumping stations, culverts, and single span bridges.

Route & Topographic Surveying

Watershed Planning

Permitting
