MWRDGC Flood Control Studies, Upper Salt Creek and Deer Creek

As part of the MWRDGC Watershed Planning efforts, two flood control projects were chosen for final engineering. FluidClarity, as a subconsultant to Black and Veatch, provided survey, wetlands/ecological services, prepared an ecological and habitat restoration planning and permitting assistance.

Survey: FluidClarity prepared the base map for the project; established benchmarks; identified utilities; conducted topographic survey. Survey tasks also included easement and property owner identification, including obtaining parcel boundary information; preparation of initial title report; and preparation of legal temporary and permanent easement descriptions on each parcel.

Wetlands/Ecological Review: FluidClarity conducted a wetlands delineation within the project area, and conducted a final ecological review within the Upper Salt Creek project area to check for threatened or endangered species habitats. Databases were also checked for archeologically sensitive areas.

Permitting and Regulatory Coordination: FluidClarity prepared permit applications and letters of intent for submission to the regulatory agencies, and aided the prime with the permit application process.
