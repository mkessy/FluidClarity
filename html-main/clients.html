Our Clients

FluidClarity is proud to serve individuals (land owners), groups (non-profit NGOs, developers, businesses) and Agencies (local municipalities, state and government agencies).

All have a stake in better watersheds, or to develop land in a responsible manner. This diverse client base allows us to be balanced in its approach and well versed in key issues, to the benefit of all clients.
